Context

Artistik was my first UX project ever to work on as part of my SVC UX certificate program.

I developed a concept project for an e-commerce art supply store that also provides free online painting tutorials. 

Artisitk is an online community that sells painting supplies and monthly subscription boxes, as well as offering free video tutorials. It allows aspiring painters to pursue their hobbies without wasting time going out for supplies and looking for classes.

Bringing the art store experience online

Problem

The concept behind this project occurred to me because of Covid-19 emerging. I wanted to have an online space for aspiring painters or beginners who want to paint for fun or people who want to meet up for a virtual group painting session or even just buy painting supplies!

I wanted to give them an interactive experience that is similar to an in-person one. 

Design Opportunity

Design a compelling end-to-end experience that encourages users to support small businesses rather than big conglomerates.

Business Goals

Sell painting supplies, offer monthly subscription boxes, teach classes, create a community. 

Research

To better understand the problem I conducted semi-structured interviews with 6 potential users.

  • 5/6 users liked the idea of learning online while still being able to interact and socialize with other aspiring painters. 

  • 3/6 like to try out the painting supplies before buying them. 

  • "Following an online tutorial will make it so much easier for us and it could even be a group activity"

Competitive Analysis

I conducted a competitive analysis with similar websites in the market. I learned that many of these websites incorporate social media (Facebook, Instagram) as a channel of communication between members where they share their art and provide feedback. I was also able to learn that they focus on offering subscription boxes.

Card Sorting

After conducting an analysis of other competitors in the market, I created the site map using a card sort method.

Wireframes

At the beginning of my design process, I created desktop wireframes based on my research insights using Figma.

UI Design

I picked colors that represent creativity and go with the overall theme.

User Testing

I did usability testing with 5 participants and this was some of the feedback I received:

  • "Love the look and feel of the website"

  • "It's so easy to navigate"

  • "I like that there are support and chat services provided"
