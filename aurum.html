Introduction

Introduction

Aurum is a premier fashion house offering luxury clothing, footwear, and accessories from top designers and artisans around the world.

Our website provides a user-friendly shopping experience, with a wide range of high-quality products and exceptional customer service.

The word "Aurum" means gold in Latin, and it could connote luxury and prestige.

User Research

User Research

After I finished designing the Website, I gave it to seven of my friends to test and provide feedback on.

Reasearch Objectve

  • To understand users' expectations and preferences for a luxury fashion website.

  • To identify the key pain points and areas for improvement in the user experience.

  • To gain insights into users' attitudes and perceptions towards the brand and its products.

  • To identify opportunities for enhancing the website's design and functionality.

Interview insights

  • Users are looking for a website that provides a seamless shopping experience with easy navigation, clear product descriptions, and an intuitive checkout process.

  • Many users find it important to have high-quality product images and detailed product descriptions that include sizing and material information.

  • Users are interested in seeing a range of products from different designers and brands, as well as exclusive products that are not available elsewhere.

  • Users are looking for a website that aligns with the brand's identity and reputation for luxury fashion.

Key objectives

  • Design a homepage that features the brand's visual identity and highlights key product categories.

  • Create a navigation system that allows users to easily browse and search for products.

  • Incorporate high-quality product photography and detailed product descriptions to inform and inspire purchase decisions.

  • Ensure that the checkout process is clear and user-friendly, with a focus on minimizing cart abandonment.

Design Challenge

Create a visually striking and user-friendly website for a luxury fashion brand that showcases the brand's unique identity and product offerings while providing a seamless shopping experience for users.

Target Audience

Fashionable people aged 17-45 prioritize quality, craftsmanship, and exclusivity in their fashion choices. They view luxury goods as a status symbol and prefer shopping online, but also value personalized customer service.

Impact

Build brand awareness and recognition, Drive engagement and interest, Build trust and credibility, Drive conversions and sales

Ideate & Test

Design Concepts

Developing multiple design concepts for the website, such as minimalistic, bold, and playful.

Navigation

Creating different navigation options, such as dropdown menus, hamburger menus, or sticky menus, and test which one works best with the target audience in terms of ease of use, clarity, and intuitiveness.

Content

Developing different types of content, such as product descriptions, videos, blog posts, and influencer collaborations, and test which ones are most engaging and effective in driving conversions.

User Experience

Testing the website's user experience, including load time, page speed, to ensure a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

Visual Design

Testing different visual design elements such as color schemes, font styles, and imagery, to determine which design elements are most effective in conveying the brand identity and engaging the target audience.

Conversion Rate Optimization

Testing different calls-to-action, checkout processes, and product pricing to optimize conversion rates and drive more sales.
