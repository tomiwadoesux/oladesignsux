Project Overview

Project Background

Hon Odedo & Co approached us to modernize their real estate operations and establish a stronger digital presence in Abuja's competitive property market. As the project manager, I led the complete digital transformation initiative that included website development, CRM integration, and operational process optimization.

The project scope encompassed property listing management, client communication systems, lead generation optimization, and establishing trust-building mechanisms for diaspora clients. I coordinated between technical teams, business stakeholders, and external vendors to deliver a comprehensive solution that positioned Hon Odedo as a leading digital-first real estate agency.

Business Analysis

Market Research & Requirements

I conducted comprehensive market analysis to understand Hon Odedo's competitive positioning and identify digital transformation opportunities in Abuja's real estate sector.

Business Challenges Identified

  • Manual property listing processes causing delays in time-to-market

  • Limited online presence reducing visibility to potential clients

  • Inefficient lead management resulting in lost opportunities

  • Lack of trust-building mechanisms for diaspora clients

  • Inconsistent client communication across different property types

Strategic Objectives

  • Reduce property listing time from 3 days to same-day publishing

  • Increase online lead generation by 200% within 6 months

  • Implement automated client communication workflows

  • Establish transparent pricing and progress tracking systems

  • Create dedicated diaspora client support infrastructure

Project Scope

Digital Platform: Full-service real estate website with property search, agent profiles, and client portal functionality for Abuja market.

Target Metrics

Performance Goals: 300% increase in online inquiries, 50% faster property transactions, 95% client satisfaction rating.

Market Impact

Positioning Hon Odedo as Abuja's premier digital-first real estate agency with specialized diaspora services and transparent transaction processes.

Implementation

Technical Implementation

I managed the development of a comprehensive real estate platform featuring advanced property search, virtual tour integration, and automated lead management. The platform required careful coordination between UI/UX designers, backend developers, and content management specialists.

Process Optimization

  • Implemented digital property listing workflows reducing upload time by 85%

  • Established automated client communication sequences for different property types

  • Created specialized diaspora client onboarding processes with video consultations

  • Integrated third-party valuation tools and market analytics for transparent pricing

Team Coordination & Quality Management

I coordinated cross-functional teams including web developers, content creators, SEO specialists, and real estate agents. The project required extensive stakeholder management to balance technical capabilities with business requirements while ensuring compliance with real estate regulations and best practices.
