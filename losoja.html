Introduction

Introduction

Lo-Soja is an innovative mobile application that has been designed with the aim of simplifying the process of buying food in bulk. Understanding that the traditional process of going to the market can be stressful and time-consuming, which is why we have created a platform that makes it easier for people to purchase essential food items without having to leave the comfort of their homes or offices.

The app has been carefully designed to provide users with an efficient and hassle-free shopping experience. With Lo-Soja, you can browse a wide range of food products, select your preferred items, and place your order in just a few clicks.

User Research

User Research

I tracked down 20 market regulars and asked them about their experiences buying in bulk at the market. 

Reasearch Objectve

  • Find out what frustrated them the most about going to the market. 

  • Determine whether they can rely on someone else to purchase their foodstuff.

  • Find out if it is easy to buy foodstuff in bulk. 

  • Find out if they can buy everything on their market list in a single trip to the market. 

Interview insights

  • 90% of them said going to the market takes time, energy, and is frustrating. 

  • 75% agreed to rely on someone else to buy their foodstuff, while 25% said they need assurance that their foodstuff will be as expected.

  • They all agreed that buying foodstuff in bulk is simple if you know the people you're buying from. 

  • 95% said they don't buy everything on their list because they don't have enough time. 

  • They all agreed that they may not want to rely on someone else to buy their foodstuffs because they don't know how long the person will take. 

Design Challenge

How can I ensure that every food item ordered is delivered on time? How will I ensure that every food item purchased meets the expectations of the customer?

Target Audience

Main audience: Expats in Lagos and Abuja who purchase food in bulk.

Impact

When this app is released, there will be trusted marketers who will buy the foodstuffs and deliver them to the various destinations.

Ideate & Test

Research

I began thinking about what I could do to assure them that their foodstuffs would be what they expected and how they could track the shopper after identifying the major problems they had, which were not being able to trust people to buy their foodstuffs and not knowing how long the shopper would take.

Solutions

  • Allowing users access to the shopper's communication details so that they can explain in depth if desired.

  • Allowing users to see the estimated time it will take the shopper.

  • Users will be able to track the shopper's location.

Competitive Analysis

Because the application is unique, it will grow quickly because it is a problem solver. Because there is no app that does what the app does, the app competition will be very low when it first launches.
