About the app

Serenity is a meditation app that offers users a wide range of guided meditations, mindful exercices, and relaxation techniques to help cultivate mindfullnes, reduce sress, and improve overall well-being. The app is designed to provide users wih a personalized meditation experience, with features such as progress tracking, customized recommendations, and personalized meditations to help them stay motivated and engaged.

With a user-friendly interface and a variety of meditation options, Serenity is an accessible and effective tool for anyone looking to develop a regular meditation practice. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced meditator, the app offers something for everyone, from quick breathing exercices to longer guided meditations. With its emphasis on mindfulness and self-care, Serenity is an excellent resource for anyone seeking greater peace, balance, and connection in their daily life.

Prototype

Onboarding and Login Screen

Users of the meditation app have the option to either sign up or log in using their Google, Facebook, or Apple account credentials. This feature offers users a convenient and efficient way to access the app, without the need to create a separate account or remember additional login information.

Home & New Meditation Entry Screen

The app's "Home" screen offers users a range of meditation options, including suggested and personalized meditations, as well as the option to resume their previous meditation session. In addition, the "New Meditation Entry" feature allows users to begin a new meditation journey.

Favorite & Profile Screen

The "Favorites" section of the app displays sounds that the user has previously marked as favorites.

On the "Profile" screen of the app, users can track their progress, view their completed sessions, and see the techniques they have completed and those they have yet to try. Additionally, they can access their journal entries.
